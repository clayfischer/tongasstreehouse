  • Adventure with us

Share your photos and videos!

We love how our guests have shared their adventures with us, including photos and videos and even guides for others to make the most of their trip. If you see a whale breaching from the deck and are okay with sharing your picture, please do share it and let us know how you'd like to caption and attribute it! You can send the photos to us via email (tongass.treehouse@gmail.com) or you can text it to us (we provide our number the day before check-in)

Social media

We are active on Instagram and Facebook, where we share local happenings and adventures at the treehouse - both by us and our guests. We would love to attribute and tag you in anything you're okay with us sharing!

Plan your adventure

We aren't travel agents, marketers or in the hospitality business. We are just locals who love our hometown and treehouse. If you're booking with us, we are happy to help you make the most of your trip! Many times folks have had questions we didn't know how to answer, but we have tracked down the people who do know, and share what we learned. :)

Come stay with us

Right now we book through AirBnB or VRBO. In the future, we may allow direct bookings for return guests, so check with us first. :)

Contact us

tongass.treehouse@gmail.com or messaging us on AirBnB or VRBO is the quickest way to get a response. Note that AirBnB and VRBO do not allow us to type website URLs, phone numbers or email addresses until after you have booked. It's their way of avoiding people circumventing the system, it's not something we have control over.
